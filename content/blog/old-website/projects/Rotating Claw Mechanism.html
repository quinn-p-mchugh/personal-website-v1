Throughout the fall 2015 semester, my team and I created a completely automated rotating claw mechanism that was able to grab a 3D printed object from one square and place the object in another square. The claw mechanism was comprised of two solenoids, two air cylinders, laser cut parts, a unidirectional motor, and an Arduino Uno. The video below shows the mechanism in action. **A full, extensive report of the project can be found [here](https://web.archive.org/web/20170830183308/https://www.dropbox.com/s/kc8387aj4sxr2vk/Rotating%20Claw%20Mechanism%20Project%20Report.pdf?dl=0).** ---
